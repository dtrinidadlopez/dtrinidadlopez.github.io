CSC 106 Final Project


Final Project!

The purpose of our program is to have an educational app where the player gets to learn a little bit of Spanish while having a little bit of fun. The scenario implemented on the app is that the player is walking up into a bookstore and the people in the counter ask them question in Spanish and the player has to answer in Spanish as well. This educational app would let the player have a little bit of taste in the Spanish language. At the end, you will receive a certificate stating that you completed the educational app and will display with how many point you ended up with.




Instructions

You will be given a prompt from the shopkeepers. Select the correct answer from the pink boxes. Selecting the right answer will give you one point. Selecting the wrong answer will take away one point. Hovering the mouse over the yellow button will show the English translation.
